Season Tix

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Season Tix is a private application used by a small, invitation-only group to coordinate Seahawks season ticket sharing. This policy describes what information we collect and how it is used.

Who Can Use This App

Access is restricted to individuals who have been explicitly invited and approved by an administrator. This is not a public service.

Information We Collect

When you sign in, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (provided by Google or entered directly)
  • Your Google account profile information if you sign in with Google

When you use the app, we also store:

  • Ticket claims you make, including which seat sets you selected and when
  • Account status (pending or approved) set by an administrator

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the ticket-sharing app — to identify you, display your name to other group members, and track which tickets you have claimed.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of this application.

Data Storage

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. All data is stored in the United States.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you are a member of the group. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting an administrator.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the app administrator at jeff_west@hotmail.com.
